[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Shadows dancing on the hardwood floor
I pull you closer than I ever did before
Lost inside the rhythm of your midnight sigh
There’s a fire burning in your steady eye

[Verse 1]
The porch light flickers as the shadows play
I wouldn't want to spend my evening any other way
I feel the sudden trace of your fingertips on mine
And every single second tastes like summer wine

[Pre-Chorus]
I feel the quiet magic in the air
I run my fingers through your messy hair
My heart is racing as I pull you near
I want to whisper every secret in your ear

[Chorus]
Oh, these tender moments in the faded light
Wrapped up in the magic of a quiet night
I need the warmth of your skin on my own
With you I’ll never have to walk this road alone

[Post-Chorus]
Yeah, holding onto you is all I know
Let the steady embers in the ashes glow

[Verse 2]

A quiet promise that I’m never going to break
